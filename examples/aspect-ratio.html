How to Calculate Padding

The aspect ratio is maintained using padding-bottom percentage:

  • 16:9 = (9 / 16) × 100 = 56.25%
  • 4:3 = (3 / 4) × 100 = 75%
  • 1:1 = (1 / 1) × 100 = 100%
  • 21:9 = (9 / 21) × 100 = 42.86%
  • Custom (width:height) = (height / width) × 100
